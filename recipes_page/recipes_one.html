Linopot(Wraped Rice)

Ingredients

  • Rice(Huma Rice or Normal Rice) (Measurement of rice according to personal preference)
  • Cassava or Purple/White Sweet Potato or Sweet Pumpkin (Option according to individual taste)
  • Salt
  • Sufficient Water (To Cook Rice)
  • Banana Leaf or Tarap Leaf or Tampu Leaf.

Steps

  1. Clean the sweet potato and thinly slice it, drain the water and set aside.
  2. Clean and wash the rice.
  3. Add sweet potato slices and cook together with rice like normal rice.
  4. Put a pinch - two of salt as seasoning.
  5. After the rice is cooked, mix the rice with a wooden spoon and mash the yams.
  6. Wash and clean the leaves, then wipe dry
  7. Place the rice on the leaves and wrap and Linopot rice is ready to be served.
